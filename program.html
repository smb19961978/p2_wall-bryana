Afterschool Programs

After-School Hours:

Monday-Friday 2:30 pm-6 pm

Thanks to a generous sponsorship from The Children's Board of Hillsborough County, we can offer FREE programming (Arts-Integrated Tutoring, Girls Circle, and Robotics) and FREE transportation from local schools for girls in 6th,7th, and 8th grades.

Girls 16 and up can volunteer with us for community service hours. If volunteering for 10 or more hours a background screening is required.

Young Risers

Every month a middle school girl from our program is chosen to be Young Riser of the month. This award is presented to the girl who best exemplifies the values and principles of the Centre for Girls at home, school, and in the community!
